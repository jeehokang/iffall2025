About the Honda Civic

A compact car that became one of the world's best-selling vehicles.

The Honda Civic is a series of automobiles manufactured by Honda since 1972.

As of 2023, it's positioned between the Honda Fit/Jazz and the Honda Accord in Honda's global passenger car lineup.

It's one of the best-selling vehicles in history, with over 27 million units sold worldwide through 2021.

Design Through the Years

Global Presence

Manufactured by Honda. Driven worldwide. Recognized everywhere.

The Civic is manufactured by Honda, a Japanese company known for innovation and reliability. Honda first became a major automaker in the 1960s with compact cars like the N360 and later the Honda 1300, leading to the Civic's debut in 1972.

The Civic has been rebadged and produced internationally, with regional versions like the Acura EL (Canada), Rover 200 (UK), and Isuzu Gemini (Japan). It's sold worldwide, and has consistently been the most popular passenger car in Canada.

The Civic is available globally in a range of trims and models, typically priced between $23,000 - $32,000 USD (2024 model year range based on Honda's official site).
